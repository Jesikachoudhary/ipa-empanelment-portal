# IPA Center of Excellence - Applicant Guide

## Complete Application Process for Expression of Interest

Welcome to the IPA Center of Excellence Expression of Interest Portal. This guide walks you through the complete process of submitting your application.

---

## PART 1: Account Setup

### Step 1: Create Account
- **Action**: Register with your email and create a password
- **Location**: Click "CREATE NEW ACCOUNT" button on the landing page
- **What You Need**:
  - Valid email address
  - Strong password (recommended: mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols)
- **Time Required**: 2-3 minutes

### Step 2: Verify Email
- **Action**: Check your email for verification link
- **Process**:
  1. After registration, you'll receive a verification email
  2. Click the verification link in the email
  3. Complete the email verification process
  4. Your account will be activated
- **Note**: You cannot login until your email is verified
- **Time Required**: 5-10 minutes (depending on email delivery)

### Step 3: Sign In
- **Action**: Login with your email and password
- **Location**: Click "SIGN IN" button or go to `/admin/login`
- **What You'll Need**:
  - Email address used during registration
  - Password you created
- **After Login**: You'll be redirected to the application form
- **Time Required**: 1 minute

---

## PART 2: Fill Application (Single Form - All Fields on One Page)

Once you're logged in, you'll see a comprehensive form with the following sections:

### Section 1: Personal Information ✎️
**Required Fields** (marked with *)

- **Full Name** *
  - Enter your complete legal name
  - Example: John Doe

- **Email Address** *
  - Your registered email address
  - Will be auto-filled from your account

- **Contact Number** *
  - Valid phone number
  - Format: 10-digit Indian phone number
  - Example: 9876543210

- **Address** *
  - Complete residential address
  - Include street, city, state, and postal code
  - Example: 123 Main Street, New Delhi, Delhi 110001

**Time Required**: 2-3 minutes

---

### Section 2: Categories & Expertise 📋
**Required Fields** (marked with *)

This section allows you to specify your areas of expertise for the Center of Excellence.

- **Select Main Categories** *
  - Choose one or more main expertise areas
  - Examples: Sports Science, Sports Management, Physical Education, etc.
  - You can select multiple categories based on your expertise

- **Choose Subcategories** *
  - For each main category selected, choose relevant subcategories
  - Subcategories are specific focus areas within main categories
  - All subcategories are required for selected categories

**Tips**:
- Select categories that align with your professional experience
- Choose as many as applicable to your expertise
- Subcategories help identify your specific areas of focus

**Time Required**: 3-5 minutes

---

### Section 3: Educational Qualifications 🎓
**Required Fields** (marked with *)

Add all your educational qualifications. You can add multiple entries.

**For Each Entry**:

- **Qualification/Degree** *
  - Type of degree/qualification
  - Examples: Bachelor of Science, Master of Arts, B.Tech, MBA, etc.
  - Max length: 100 characters

- **Institution/University** *
  - Name of the institution where you studied
  - Examples: Delhi University, IIT Delhi, Jawaharlal Nehru University, etc.
  - Max length: 150 characters

- **Passing Year** *
  - Year when you completed the qualification
  - Format: YYYY (e.g., 2020)
  - Must be a valid year

**Adding Multiple Entries**:
- Click "Add Education" button to add another qualification
- You can add as many educational qualifications as you have
- All entries will be submitted together
- Each entry requires all three fields to be filled

**Tips**:
- Add qualifications in chronological order (oldest to most recent)
- Include all degrees and certifications
- Be accurate with institution names and years

**Time Required**: 3-5 minutes

---

### Section 4: Professional Experience 💼
**Required Fields**: This section is optional but recommended

Add your professional work experience. You can add multiple entries.

**For Each Entry**:

- **Organization Name**
  - Name of the company/organization where you worked
  - Example: XYZ Sports Academy, ABC Fitness Center, etc.
  - Max length: 150 characters

- **Role/Designation**
  - Your job title or position
  - Examples: Head Coach, Sports Manager, Physical Trainer, etc.
  - Max length: 100 characters

- **From Year & Month**
  - When you started this position
  - Format: YYYY-MM (e.g., 2019-01)
  - Select from dropdown

- **To Year & Month**
  - When you ended this position
  - Format: YYYY-MM (e.g., 2023-12)
  - Select from dropdown
  - Must be after "From" date

- **Details**
  - Description of your responsibilities and achievements
  - Key projects, contributions, or achievements
  - Max length: 500 characters
  - Example: "Managed team of 15 coaches, organized national-level tournaments, improved athlete performance by 25%"

**Adding Multiple Entries**:
- Click "Add Experience" button to add another experience entry
- Add all relevant professional experiences
- Each entry requires all fields to be completed

**Tips**:
- Include all relevant professional experience
- Provide detailed descriptions of your role and contributions
- Most recent experience should be listed first
- Be specific about achievements and responsibilities

**Time Required**: 5-10 minutes

---

### Section 5: Documents (Optional) 📄
**Optional Fields**

Upload supporting documents to strengthen your application.

- **Resume/CV**
  - Accepted Formats: PDF, DOC, DOCX
  - Maximum File Size: 2 MB
  - Recommended: Single-page resume with key highlights
  - Include: Contact info, education, experience, skills, certifications

- **Additional Document**
  - Any supporting document that strengthens your application
  - Accepted Formats: PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, PNG
  - Maximum File Size: 5 MB
  - Examples: Certificates, awards, publications, project highlights, etc.

**Upload Tips**:
- Ensure file names are clear and descriptive
- Check file size before uploading
- Files should be in good quality and legible
- Document upload is optional but recommended

**Time Required**: 2-3 minutes (if uploading documents)

---

### Section 6: Final Step - Submit ✅
**Review & Submit**

Before submitting your application:

1. **Review All Information**
   - Check all personal details for accuracy
   - Verify all required fields are filled
   - Confirm categories and expertise selections
   - Review education and experience entries

2. **Verify Documents** (if uploaded)
   - Ensure documents are properly uploaded
   - Check file formats and sizes

3. **Submit Application**
   - Click the "Submit" button
   - Your application will be submitted to the system
   - You'll receive a confirmation message

**Important Notes**:
- ⚠️ This is a **ONE-TIME SUBMISSION**
- ⚠️ Once submitted, your application **CANNOT BE EDITED** later
- ⚠️ Make sure all information is correct before submitting
- ✓ You'll receive a confirmation email after successful submission

**Time Required**: 1-2 minutes

---

## Total Application Time

- **Part 1 (Account Setup)**: 8-15 minutes (includes email verification wait time)
- **Part 2 (Application Form)**: 10-15 minutes
- **Total**: 18-30 minutes

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I edit my application after submitting?**
- A: No, the system does not allow edits after submission. Please review carefully before clicking Submit.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
- A: Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page and follow the email instructions to reset it.

**Q: How long does email verification take?**
- A: Usually within 5-10 minutes. Check spam folder if not in inbox.

**Q: What file formats are accepted for documents?**
- A: Resume: PDF, DOC, DOCX (max 2MB). Additional: PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, PNG (max 5MB).

**Q: Can I add more experiences/educations than shown?**
- A: Yes, you can add as many as needed using the "Add" buttons.

**Q: Is uploading documents mandatory?**
- A: No, documents are optional. But uploading a resume is recommended.

**Q: What happens after I submit?**
- A: Your application is reviewed by the selection committee. You'll be notified via email about the next steps.
